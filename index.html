Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing
Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

Step 1/8: Raw Material

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution
Step 2/8: Extrusion

Precision Extrusion System

Our advanced extruders melt the raw material evenly, maintaining strict temperature profiles for structurally flawless pipes.

  • Automated gravimetric feeding
  • Advanced screw geometry
Step 3/8: Cooling

Vacuum Calibration & Cooling

Intensive spray cooling tanks rapidly bring the extruded pipe down to shape without inducing thermal stress.

  • Multi-stage cooling zones
  • Dimensional stability locks
Step 4/8: Sizing

Laser Sizing & Measurement

Real-time laser micrometers continuously scan the pipe circumference to ensure exacting OD and wall density.

  • Inline continuous scanning
  • Zero-tolerance variance
Step 5/8: Quality Control

Stringent Quality Control

Batch specimens undergo severe hydrostatic, tensile, and melt-flow index tests in our state-of-the-art lab.

  • 100% burst pressure tested
  • Exceeds IS 4984 standards
Step 6/8: Marking

Laser Marking & Traceability

Each meter is permanently laser-etched with batch info, sizing, and standards for complete field traceability.

  • Friction-resistant marking
  • Lifetime batch tracking
Step 7/8: Cutting

Clean Precision Cutting

Planetary cutting saws seamlessly slice continuous runs into precise custom lengths without dust or burrs.

  • Burr-free clean cuts
  • Automated length sensing
Step 8/8: Packaging

Coiling & Dispatch Packaging

Flexible diameters are neatly coiled while straight lengths are bundled securely to prevent transport damage.

  • Automated tight coiling
  • UV-protected wrapping
